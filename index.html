My name is Brad Vorjohan. I work full time at Appian in the DC area, and am working on By The People, the first citizen lobbying platform!

I graduated from Virginia Tech with a degree in Mechanical Engineering in 2016. For about a year and a half I worked as a Structural Design Engineer at Lockheed Martin. In 2018 I transitioned to software and started my current role as a Application Engineer at Appian.

In my spare time, in addition to trying to become the best developer that I can, I am developing By The People with two of my friends from college. For more information, visit By The People's website.
